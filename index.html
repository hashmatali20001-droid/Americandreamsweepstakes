Rules & Disclaimers

Based on supplied prize structure and values, we draft a set of Official Sweepstakes Rules (and disclaimers, as needed), then review revisions and update as required. We treat the Official Rules of a promotion as essentially a contract between the listed promotional sponsor and the target audience. Official Rules cover everything from A to Z about the promotion including (but not limited to) eligibility, dates, entry methods, prizes, prize conditions, use of data, odds of winning, etc.

Regardless of whether you are giving away a $100 gift card or a $100,000 check, Official Rules governing the promotion are needed if you are incentivizing consumer action through the use of game of chance or game of skill. Once a promotion begins, everything in the rules is set in stone. You can not edit the dates or prizes in the rules document as the promotion progresses.
